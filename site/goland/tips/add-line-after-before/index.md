*Language-aware line adding, no muss no fuss.*

**How to use:**
Press *Shift + Enter on Windows/Linux* or *⇧ + Enter on macOS*,
and you can add a line and place the cursor on it.

Press *Ctrl + Alt + Enter on Windows/Linux* or *⌘ + ⌥ + Enter on macOS*
to add a line above.
