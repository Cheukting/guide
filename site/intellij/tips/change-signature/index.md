Press **⌘F6** (macOS), or **Ctrl+F6** (Windows/Linux), to bring up the **Change Signature** dialog. You can add or remove method parameters here.  

You can use **⌘N** (macOS), or **Alt+Ins** (Windows/Linux), to add a new parameter.
