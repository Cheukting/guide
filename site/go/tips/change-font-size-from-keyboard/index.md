To increase the font size, press <kbd>⌃⇧.</kbd> (macOS) / <kbd>Alt+Shift+Period</kbd> (Windows/Linux). To decrease it, press <kbd>⌃⇧,</kbd> (macOS) / <kbd>Alt+Shift+Comma</kbd> (Windows/Linux).
