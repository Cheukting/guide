Use the _Navigation Bar_ to move around files, even when the whole UI is hidden away.

**How to use:**

Bring the _Navigation Bar_, <kbd>⌘↑</kbd> (macOS) / <kbd>Alt+Home</kbd> (Windows/Linux).
